Channel Description

The group is designed for beginners who are eager to learn and master digital art software, including popular tools like Photoshop, Procreate, and Illustrator. It provides a supportive environment where newcomers can explore the fundamentals of digital illustration, graphic design, and photo editing. The focus is on helping users develop their skills, understand the interface, and create stunning digital artwork, whether they are aspiring to be professional artists or simply pursuing a creative hobby.

Group Rules

1.

Respect and Kindness:Always treat fellow members with respect and kindness. Harassment, hate speech, or any form of discrimination will not be tolerated.

2.

Stay On Topic:Ensure your posts and comments are relevant to the group's focus. Off-topic discussions may be removed.

3.

No Spamming: Avoid posting spam, promotional content, or irrelevant links. Keep the conversation meaningful and valuable.

4.

Constructive Criticism:When providing feedback, make sure it's constructive and helpful. We encourage growth through positive and actionable suggestions.

5.

Protect Privacy:Do not share personal information (yours or others') publicly. Respect each member's privacy.

6.

No Plagiarism:Share original content or credit the rightful owner. Plagiarism is not allowed and may lead to removal from the group.

7.

Report Issues:If you notice any behavior that violates these rules, report it to the moderators. Let's work together to maintain a healthy community.
